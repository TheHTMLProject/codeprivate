On track

What problem are we solving?

Many classrooms block the tools students need to reach The HTML Project. Classroom Companion bundles curated access links, study timers, and stealth launchers into one friendly panel.

How can you help?

Share your school device quirks, suggest shortcuts, or donate a new URL for the mirror list. Contributors are credited in our changelog.

Availability

Pilot testing is open on desktop browsers. Mobile support lands when we finish gesture navigation next sprint.
